About the Check for flooding beta service

We’re testing a new design of the Flood information service called Check for flooding. It’s currently a ‘beta’ service. This means it’s the first version of it.

You can use the new service and give us feedback to help us improve it.

Because we’re still adding new features, it might not always be available.

The existing Flood information service is available to use any time until we replace it with the Check for flooding service.

You can also call Floodline 24 hours a day by:

  • telephone on 0345 988 1188
  • textphone on 0345 602 6340

Beta services

GOV.UK regularly launches new or redesigned digital services. During the beta phase services are continually tested and improved.

The beta label is displayed on a new service to show it’s being tested - it may not work for everyone.

Sometimes a beta service will be available at the same time as an older, existing service - you can choose which one to use.

Successful beta services eventually become ‘live’ and replace any older services that perform the same task.
