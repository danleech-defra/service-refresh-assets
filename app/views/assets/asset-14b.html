About the Check for flooding beta service

We’re testing a new design of the Flood information service called Check for flooding.

It’s currently in the 'beta' phase. This means it’s the first version of it, so we're continually testing and improving it.

Use the Check for flooding service and give us feedback to help us improve it.

It might sometimes be unavailable because we’re adding new features.

We'll keep this to a minimum so you can use it to check for flooding 24 hours a day.

You can:

  • use the Flood information service right up until we replace it with Check for flooding
  • call Floodline 24 hours a day by telephone on 0345 988 1188, or text phone on textphone on 0345 602 6340
