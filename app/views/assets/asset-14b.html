About the Check for flooding beta service

We’re testing a new design of the Flood information service called Check for flooding.

It’s currently in the 'beta' phase. This means it’s the first version of it and we're continually testing and improving it.

You can use the new service and give us feedback to help us improve it.

It might not always be available because we’re still adding new features.

The existing Flood information service is available any time until we replace it with the Check for flooding service.

You can also call Floodline 24 hours a day by:

  • telephone on 0345 988 1188
  • textphone on 0345 602 6340
