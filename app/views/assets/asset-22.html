Very low risk from surface water flooding

(b) - Coastal, Low risk [low, significant]

Low risk from coastal flooding

Runoff from rainfall or blocked drains would possibly lead to significant or widespread flooding, but this is not expected. If this happens, your life might be in danger. It could also cause some travel disruption.

(c) - Rivers, Medium risk [medium, severe]

Medium risk from river flooding

High or overflowing rivers would possibly lead to severe or widespread flooding. If this happens, your life might be in danger and you should be prepared to evacuate. It could also cause severe travel disruption.
